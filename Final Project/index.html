About

As an exchange student and a traveller from Sweden I frequently find myself in conversations where my counterpart uses idiomatic expressions in order to make a point. These situations were what made me come up with the idea of creating a site where the user easily could search for, and learn, new idomatic expression. The purpose is to contribute to a deeper level understanding of the language. Happy learning!


- Tomas
